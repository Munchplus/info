About


MunchPlus offers nutritionally enriched flour that has been blended together with local ingredients to provide added health benefits for those consuming it. It also creates a positive association with nutritious and tasty food that one can enjoy at the same time.

MISSIONVISION

Mission


To reduce the high levels of under-five children malnutrition in Malawi by providing access to safe, affordable and nutritionally enriched complementary flour blends made from locally available food materials. We strive to empower families in rural areas through education and training on the production and consumption of nutritious complementary food, while promoting a positive association with tasty and healthy food. Our team's expertise in food and nutrition, product development, food processing, food analysis, and food chemistry is dedicated to achieving optimal health outcomes for all family members in Malawi.

Vision


To create a Malawi where every child has access to safe, affordable, and nutritious complementary food, thereby reducing the prevalence of under-five malnutrition. We aim to empower families with the knowledge and skills to produce their own nutritionally enriched flour blends using locally available food materials. Through our efforts, we envision a future where Malawian families are equipped with the tools and resources to make informed decisions about their health and wellbeing, and where nutritious food is recognized as a fundamental right for all.
